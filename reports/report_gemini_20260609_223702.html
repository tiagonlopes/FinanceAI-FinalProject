5. Methodology Notes

  • Data Sources: Technical data derived from daily price, volume, and indicator calculations. Fundamental data represents key financial metrics as of the provided date.
  • Forecast Models: Short-term 5-day price forecasts are generated using an ensemble approach, combining predictions from ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average) and Prophet models.
  • Forecast Agreement:
    • Converging: When ARIMA and Prophet models project similar direction and magnitude, it increases confidence in the ensemble forecast.
    • Diverging: When models predict significantly different outcomes (direction or magnitude), it indicates high uncertainty, and the ensemble forecast should be treated with caution.
  • Bullish Probability Score: A rule-based, technical-only score for a 5-day outlook, assessing the likelihood of a bullish move based on a predefined set of technical indicators.
  • Limitations: Forecasts are short-term and based on historical patterns; past performance is not indicative of future results. Fundamental data provides a snapshot and does not account for forward-looking qualitative factors or unforeseen market events.
