## 5. Methodology Notes

*   **Data Sources:** Market and fundamental data are provided as of 2026-06-09.
*   **Technical Analysis:** Utilizes common indicators including RSI, SMA, EMA, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and ADX to assess trend, momentum, and volatility.
*   **Forecast Models:**
    *   **ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average):** A statistical model for time series forecasting, suitable for capturing linear trends and seasonality.
    *   **Prophet (Facebook's Prophet):** An open-source forecasting library designed for business time series, robust to missing data and shifts in trend.
    *   **Ensemble:** A weighted average of ARIMA and Prophet forecasts, used as the primary predictive output.
    *   **Forecast Agreement:** Measures the directional consensus and magnitude similarity between ARIMA and Prophet. "Diverging" indicates significant disagreement, raising confidence concern. "Converging" indicates agreement, raising confidence.
*   **Bullish Probability (5-day):** A rule-based technical score derived from multiple indicators, providing a short-term directional lean.
*   **Limitations:** Forecasts are probabilistic and do not guarantee future performance. Market conditions can change rapidly. Fundamental data, especially dividend yield for SPY/QQQ/DIA/IWM and AAPL, appears unusually high, which may indicate a data reporting anomaly or specific special dividend situation, and should be verified independently. This report is based solely on the provided data and does not incorporate broader macroeconomic factors or qualitative analysis.
