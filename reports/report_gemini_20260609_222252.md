## 5. Methodology Notes

*   **Data Sources:** Technical indicators and fundamental metrics are derived from the provided market data as of 2026-06-09.
*   **Forecast Models:**
    *   **ARIMA (Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average):** A linear time series model suitable for data with trends and seasonality.
    *   **Prophet:** An additive time series model developed by Facebook, robust to missing data and shifts in trend.
    *   **Ensemble Forecast:** The final 5-day price forecast is the mean average of the ARIMA and Prophet model outputs, aiming to leverage the strengths of both.
*   **Forecast Agreement/Disagreement:**
    *   **Converging:** When ARIMA and Prophet project similar directional movement and magnitude, increasing confidence in the ensemble forecast.
    *   **Diverging:** When ARIMA and Prophet project significantly different outcomes (e.g., one positive, one strongly negative, or similar direction but large magnitude difference), indicating high uncertainty and warranting caution for the ensemble.
*   **Bullish Probability:** A rule-based technical score (5-day outlook) assessing the likelihood of upward price movement based purely on technical indicators. This score does not incorporate fundamental analysis or forecast model outputs.
*   **Caveats:** This report relies on historical data and predictive models, which are subject to inherent limitations and cannot guarantee future performance. Forecasts are short-term (5-day) and technical probabilities are based solely on technical patterns, without considering broader market news or qualitative factors.
